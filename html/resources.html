Websites

Lichess.org

Lichess.org is a free, open-source chess server powered by volunteers and donations.
You are able to play chess with other people from all sorts of country!
Access to opening book, analysis board, puzzles and more!
click here to visit the site










Chess.com

Chess.com is a totally free to access website for you those chess enthusiast!
You are able to watch, learn, play chess and have fun in chess.com.
You can even access to the latest news of chess!
click here to visit the site










Chessable

Chessable uses science-backed learning techniques to help boost your retention.
The courses on this website is not free though, but their instructors are best in the world!
